### Query 5: What is an SIP and how does it work?
**Answer:** A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a method of investing in mutual funds where you contribute a fixed amount regularly (monthly, quarterly, etc.) rather than a lump sum. It helps in rupee cost averaging and benefits from the power of compounding over time.

Secondary Source: [Groww Official AMC](https://groww.in/mutual-funds/amc/groww-mutual-fund)
